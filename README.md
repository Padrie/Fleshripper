# Fleshripper

![Header](https://img.itch.zone/aW1nLzIyNjA0NTkyLnBuZw==/original/kceWqH.png)

- Fleshripper is a 3D Boomer Shooter set in a bloody, gothic like environment with inspiration to old Playstation Graphics. Shoot your way through horrifying creatures and use the special ammunation and weapons you collect mindfully.

<h2 align="left">Features</h2>

- 2D Enemies in a 3D Environemnt
- 2 Weapons, the Machine gun and Shotgun
- 2 Special types of ammo, Explosion and Lightning
- A moody and atmospheric setting
- Fast paced and satisfying movement
- And an amazing Soundtrack

<h2 align="left">Contributions</h2>

This project was made in a team of 11, while on the engineering side we had 2 people, my teammate Kanefer who was responsible for Movement, Enemy AI and music, and me, that worked on Gunplay and ammunation, implementing Animations and UI

<h2 align="left">My Responsibility</h2>

<h3 align="left">Gunplay</h3>

In our game, we had two guns and two special bullets. The first gun was a machine gun that would quickly shoot bullets while having a moderate sized magazine, and the other was the shotgun, slow firing, low ammo but packs a punch and can decimate crows of enemies.
Then we also have the special bullets. The first is the Explosive bullet, that would deal an Area of effect blast, pushing enemies back, and would damage them in a radius. And the lightning bullet, that would chain lightning bolt from one enemy to another, dealing massive damage.
